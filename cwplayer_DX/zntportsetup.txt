zntportsetup.exe
================
Please run this utility program only if you intend to use the parallel (LPT) port and if my application does not automatically detect your LPT port.
After running this utility, restart your computer.
=============================================================

Après exécution de cet utilitaire, redémarrez votre ordinateur.
